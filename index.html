How to Order Using the Groce Foods App

Step 1: Download and Install the App

For iOS Users: Open the App Store, search for "Groce Foods", and tap "Download". For Android Users: Open the Google Play Store, search for "Groce Foods", and tap "Install".

Step 2: Sign Up or Log In

New Users: Tap on "Sign Up" to create a new account. Enter your email, create a password, and fill in any other required information. Existing Users: Tap on "Log In" and enter your email and password.

Step 3: Browse Products

Categories: Navigate through the categories (e.g., Fruits, Vegetables, Dairy, etc.) to find the products you need. Search: Use the search bar at the top of the screen to quickly find specific items. Filters: Apply filters to sort products by price, popularity, or dietary preferences.

Step 4: Add Items to Your Cart

Select Item: Tap on a product to view its details. Adjust Quantity: Use the + and - buttons to adjust the quantity. Add to Cart: Tap the "Add to Cart" button to add the item to your shopping cart.

Step 5: Review Your Cart

View Cart: Tap on the cart icon at the top right corner of the screen. Edit Items: Adjust quantities or remove items from your cart as needed. Apply Coupons: Enter any discount codes or coupons you have.

Step 6: Proceed to Checkout

Checkout: Tap the "Checkout" button. Delivery Details: Enter your delivery address or select from saved addresses. Delivery Time: Choose a preferred delivery time slot. Payment Method: Select your payment method (Credit/Debit Card, PayPal, etc.).

Step 7: Confirm and Place Your Order

Review Order: Double-check your order details, delivery address, and payment method. Place Order: Tap the "Place Order" button to finalize your purchase. Confirmation: You will receive an order confirmation screen and an email with your order details.

Step 8: Track Your Order

Order Status: Go to the "My Orders" section in the app to view the status of your current and

orders. Live Tracking: For real-time updates, tap on your current order to see the delivery progress. past

Step 9: Receive Your Delivery

Delivery Notification: You will receive a notification when your order is out for delivery. Receive Order: Accept your order from the delivery person and verify the items.

Step 10: Provide Feedback

Rate Your Experience: After receiving your order, you will be prompted to rate your experience and provide feedback. Customer Support: If you encounter any issues, contact our customer support through the app for assistance.
