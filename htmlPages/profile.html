Welcome to Groce Foods!

We're here to assist you with any questions or issues you might have. Below, you'll find answers to common questions and guidance on using our app effectively.

Creating an Account

  • Open the Groce Foods app
  • Tap on the "Sign Up" button.
  • Enter your email address, create a password, and provide other necessary details.
  • Verify your email address through the verification link sent to your inbox.

Logging In

  • Open the app and tap on the "Log In" button.
  • TEnter your registered email and password.
  • ap "Log In" to access your account.

Payment

  • Choose your preferred payment method (credit card, debit card, or digital wallet).
  • Enter the required payment details and tap "Confirm Payment."

Updating Profile Information

  • Go to the "Account" section.
  • Tap on "Profile" and update your personal details.

Changing Password

  • In the "Account" section, select "Change Password."
  • Enter your current password and the new password, then tap "Save."
